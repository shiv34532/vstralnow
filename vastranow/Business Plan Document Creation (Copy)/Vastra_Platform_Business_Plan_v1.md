## 5. Policies

### 5.1 Shipping Policy
- Free shipping on orders above ₹999
- Standard shipping: ₹99 (3-7 business days)
- Express shipping: ₹199 (1-3 business days)
- Ships across India
- Order tracking available
- Delivery partners: Delhivery, Blue Dart

### 5.2 Return & Refund Policy (Initial Phase)
**NO RETURNS POLICY - Phase 1**

Due to the nature of clothing and hygiene considerations, we currently do not accept returns or exchanges.

**Please ensure:**
- Check size chart carefully before ordering
- Review product images and description
- Contact customer support for any queries before purchase

**Exceptions:**
- Damaged products on arrival (report within 24 hours with photos)
- Wrong product delivered (report within 24 hours)
- Manufacturing defects (report within 24 hours)

In case of valid exceptions, we will offer:
- Full refund to original payment method
- Replacement (subject to stock availability)

**Future Phase:** 7-day exchange policy for select categories.

### 5.3 Cancellation Policy
- Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement
- After 2 hours, cancellation depends on order status
- If order is shipped, cancellation not possible
- Refund processed within 5-7 business days

### 5.4 Privacy Policy Highlights
- Customer data encrypted and secure
- No data shared with third parties without consent
- Payment information not stored
- Email communications opt-in based
- Compliance with Indian data protection laws

## 7. Pricing Strategy

### 7.1 Pricing Tiers

**Economy Range:** ₹399 - ₹799
- Basic cotton kurtas
- Simple designs
- Limited color options
- Target: Budget-conscious buyers

**Mid-Range:** ₹800 - ₹1,999
- Better quality fabrics
- More design variety
- Multiple color options
- Target: Primary customer segment

**Premium Range:** ₹2,000 - ₹5,000+
- Designer pieces
- Silk, premium fabrics
- Intricate work and embellishments
- Target: Occasion/wedding shoppers

### 7.2 Competitive Pricing
- 10-20% lower than premium brands (Fabindia, Biba)
- Comparable to Myntra/Ajio ethnic wear
- Better quality than fast fashion platforms

### 7.3 Promotional Strategy
- Launch offer: 20% off first order
- Festival sales: Up to 40% off
- Referral program: ₹200 off for referrer and referee
- Loyalty points: 1 point per ₹100 spent
- Free shipping on orders above ₹999

## 8. Competitor Analysis

### Direct Competitors

**1. Fabindia**
- Strengths: Brand reputation, quality, store presence
- Weaknesses: Higher pricing, limited online experience
- Our Advantage: Better pricing, modern designs

**2. Biba**
- Strengths: Women's ethnic wear specialist, good designs
- Weaknesses: Higher price point
- Our Advantage: Serves entire family, competitive pricing

**3. Manyavar (Men's)**
- Strengths: Strong brand, occasion wear specialist
- Weaknesses: Limited casual/office wear
- Our Advantage: Wider range across occasions

**4. W for Woman**
- Strengths: Contemporary designs, good online presence
- Weaknesses: Premium pricing
- Our Advantage: Traditional + contemporary, better value

### Online Marketplaces

**Myntra, Ajio, Amazon Fashion**
- Strengths: Huge selection, competitive pricing, quick delivery
- Weaknesses: Overwhelming choice, quality inconsistency, no brand story
- Our Advantage: Curated collection, quality assurance, brand narrative

### Our Differentiation
1. **Quality at Accessible Prices:** Premium feel without premium pricing
2. **Curated Collections:** Carefully selected designs, not overwhelming
3. **Family Focus:** One-stop shop for entire family's ethnic and formal needs
4. **Authentic Indian Wear:** Focus on traditional craftsmanship with modern fits
5. **Customer Service:** Personalized support, styling advice

## 10. Technology Stack

### Option A: Easy Start (Recommended for Launch)
- **Platform:** WordPress + WooCommerce
- **Hosting:** Hostinger (₹149/month)
- **Domain:** vastra.com or vastra.in
- **Payment Gateway:** Razorpay, PayU
- **Shipping Integration:** Shiprocket
- **Cost:** ~₹10,000 setup + ₹2,000/month operational

### Option B: Custom Development (Future Scale)
- **Frontend:** React + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Node.js/Python Django
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Hosting:** AWS/DigitalOcean
- **Cost:** ~₹50,000 development + ₹5,000/month hosting

## 12. Operations Plan

### Inventory Management
**Phase 1:** Made-to-order/Dropshipping model
- Partner with manufacturers
- No inventory holding
- 7-10 day delivery time

**Phase 2:** Hybrid model
- Stock best-sellers
- Made-to-order for specialty items
- 3-5 day delivery for stock items

### Suppliers
- Source from: Surat (fabrics), Jaipur (ethnic wear), Delhi (manufacturing)
- Quality control: Sample approval before bulk orders
- MOQ negotiations for better pricing

### Logistics
- Partner with Shiprocket for multi-courier access
- Packaging: Branded boxes/pouches
- Include: Thank you card, care instructions, discount code for next purchase

## 15. Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Initial Traffic
- **Mitigation:** Aggressive social media marketing, influencer partnerships, SEO optimization

### Risk 2: Product Quality Issues
- **Mitigation:** Strict vendor selection, quality checks, clear return policy for defects

### Risk 3: High Return Rates (Future Phase)
- **Mitigation:** Detailed size guides, accurate product photography, virtual try-on (future)

### Risk 4: Cash Flow Issues
- **Mitigation:** Start with made-to-order model, advance payment from customers

### Risk 5: Competition
- **Mitigation:** Focus on niche (family ethnic wear), superior customer service, community building
